HOW TO MAKE A CIRCUIT

To make a circuit, first you need a source of energy, like a battery; something that conducts electricity like a copper wire, and finally for this tutorial, a light bulb.


A circuit needs two things: A path for electricity to flow from positive and negative (like the + and - on a battery!), and it needs a supply of that energy.

The simplest way we can make a circuit is by attaching one end of a wire to any side of the battery, wrapping that on the bottom of the light bulb, and putting the metal bottom of the light bulb on the other side of the battery!


You can play around with this by putting things in between the battery and the end of the light bulb, such as a paper clip of a ball of paper. Notice what happens; if the flow is stopped, the material is an insulator. if the flow of energy keeps going, its a conductor.
